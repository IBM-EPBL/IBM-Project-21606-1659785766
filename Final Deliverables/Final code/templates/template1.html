INVENTORY MANAGEMENT



Inventory Management System for Retailers is a cloud based web application used to track and manage inventory for Retailers. In this the retailer can products by entering the product details such as product id, product name , product price, quantity of the product and the image url to display the product in the cart page. Once the product is added, the added product is displayed in the cart page and the product is available for the customer to purchase. later the products can be bought from the cart page. For that the customer need to create an account by entering the email id and password.
